Cookies Policy

When browsing the internet, in some cases, residual elements such as temporary files, also called cookies, are stored on your computer or device. Each cookie is unique to your browser and contains specific anonymous information such as the identifier and name of the web page accompanied by figures and numbers.

These elements are addressed to improve the user's experience when surfing the network by enabling data retrieval on subsequent visits and storage of their preferences to surf more efficiently, quickly and simply.

TYPES OF COOKIES USED

OWN: are those sent to the user's final device, from a device or domain managed by the editor itself, and by the one who provides the service requested by the user.

THIRD-PARTY: are those sent to the user's end device, from a device or domain whose management is not done by the editor, but by another entity responsible for the data obtained through cookies.

SESSION: are cookies designed to obtain and store data while the user accesses a web page.

PERSISTENT: are cookies in which data remains stored on the device and can be accessed and processed for a period determined by the person responsible for the cookie. The period extends from a few minutes to many years.

OF ANALYSIS: are those handled by us and by third parties, which allow us to quantify the number of users and, thus, carry out the measurement and statistical analysis of the use of users of the service offered. Therefore, we analyze your navigation on our website in order to improve the offer of products or services that we are offering.

ADVERTISING: when you visit web pages or open emails in which advertisements or promotions are published about our products or services, a cookie will be installed on your browser, which will later serve to display advertising associated with your searches and to develop a control of advertisements related, for example, to the number of times they are seen, where they appear, at what time, etc.
